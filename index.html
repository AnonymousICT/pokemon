Pokemon Damage Relation Search

I made this web app because I, personally, could never remember what would one-shot (deal damage to my Pokemon to reduce its health from full to zero) my Pokemon. This web app uses pokeapi.co to display the pokedex number, the Pokemon's name, the type(s) of the Pokemon and the defensive damage relationships from all the attack types in the Pokemon video game universe. - Howard Kuo

Click on any Pokeball to change theme
